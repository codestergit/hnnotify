Privacy Statement

HN Notify is a free service I am providing to the Hacker News community.

HN is one of the few online communities I really enjoy participating in and reply-notification is something I have wanted to have for HN for years now.

Your email address and HN username are stored in a local DB and used by the service to determine who gets notifications of new messages.

That is it.

I will not sell your email address or share it with any outside source.
